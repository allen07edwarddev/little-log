Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

LittleLog (“the app”) is built to help parents track their baby's daily life. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it lives, and what permissions it uses.

TL;DR

  • All your data stays on your device. LittleLog does not have a server. No accounts, no sign-in, no cloud sync.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage statistics or personal information.
  • Photos and notes you add (diaper checks, milestones, etc.) are stored only in the app's private storage on your phone.
  • Backups are exported manually by you, and only when you tap “Share backup”. Nothing leaves your device automatically.

What data we handle

LittleLog stores the following on-device:

  • Baby profile info you enter (name, date of birth, optional sex, optional birth weight/length, optional photo)
  • Daily logs you record (diapers, bottles, breast feeds, sleep, solids, temperature, custom categories, free-text notes)
  • Photos you attach to logs or milestone months
  • Medication schedules and dose history
  • App preferences (theme, custom categories, alarm sounds)

All of this is saved in a local SQLite database and the app's private files directory. We never transmit it to a server we control.

Sharing & backups

LittleLog includes a “Backup” feature that exports your data as a single file (JSON + photos in a zip). The export is created locally and is only sent somewhere if you choose to share it via your phone's share sheet (e.g., to email, cloud drive, or another device). We never receive a copy.

Milestone collages can be shared as PNG images via the share sheet — same model: we hand the file to your share sheet and step out of the way.

Children's privacy

LittleLog is designed for parents and caregivers to track their own child's data. The app is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the “Last updated” date at the top.
